The world of cloud was so small,
Liked floating in the air, dim and dull.
Too vast seeing the sky to chase the goal,
Till dreamed of a cedar with a beautiful soul.

She stood high and saw far,
Catched the lonely cloud across the state.
Miss was transferred by the warm sunlight,
Like gentle strokes through the cheek line.

Cloud was inspired by the favor,
And put all effort to drift over.
Felt like an invisible connection bridges the hearts,
Hit by the Cupid’s arrow and got scattered.

Cedar kept standing in the cold wind,
Though she could not stand the cold.
Cuz she knew, one day her cloud would be brought,
Through the crowd,
To intertwine with her being proud,
No matter how long they would hold out.
Hazel, this is how long we've been in love with each other.
As soulful as the confession,
as long as the acompanying.
—— Yunyi
